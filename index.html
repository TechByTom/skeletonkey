Skeleton Key by davidarnold

A simple bookmarklet for generating site-specific passwords.

When you need to enter a password, click the bookmarklet link, enter a long passphrase at the prompt, and hit OK. Paste the displayed password into the page's password field.

Dependencies

Tested on Firefox, Chrome, and Safari. Known not to work on Internet Explorer due to several browser deficiencies.

Install

Simply add this link to your bookmarks: Site Password
