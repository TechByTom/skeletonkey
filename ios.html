Skeleton Key

The mobile version of Safari does not support saving bookmarklets directly. We'll have to trick it into doing the right thing.

Instructions for iOS devices

  1. Tap inside the block of text below to bring up the on-screen keyboard, tap again to open the context menu, choose Select All and then Copy.
  2. Bookmark this page.
  3. Go into the bookmarks menu and edit the new Site Password bookmark.
  4. Tap inside the address field and then tap the ⦻ icon to clear the existing address text.
  5. Tap inside the now empty address field and choose Paste.
  6. Exit out of the bookmark menu.
  7. Phew! You're done!
